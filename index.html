Not only people need a house Our goal is to find a suitable friend for everyone. It doesn’t matter if you both have wool and legs, your new friend will love you with anything.
About the shelter “Cutie Pets” The city non-profit public organization "Cutie Pets" was created to organize the activities of the shelter. The purpose of the organization's activity is to regulate the number of homeless animals; keeping dogs and cats in the shelter and finding new owners for pets. The shelter started its work in 2012. In almost 10 years, we have found a home for more than 1000 dogs and 900 cats. More than 2,000 animals were operated on and saved. Every day, our employees put their strength, time and soul into each ponytail. Our team hopes that over the years of the organization's activity, they have saved not only a huge number of animals, but also have brought up in people a sense of responsibility, care and love for our smaller brothers.
